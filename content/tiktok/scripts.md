## Production Notes

- **Hook**: First 2 seconds must grab attention (bold text, question, surprising fact)
- **Captions**: Always use large, readable captions (most watch without sound)
- **Music**: Traditional Chinese instrumental or trending TikTok audio
- **CTA**: End every video with a clear call-to-action
- **Frequency**: Post 1-2 videos daily for the first 14 days
- **Best times**: 7-9 PM EST (US), 8-10 PM JST (Japan)
